Dr. Drauzio Varella

The man who gave hope to the powerless

Here's a time line of Dr. Varella's life:

  • 1943 - Born in São Paulo,SP
  • 1964 - enrolled in medicine at the Medical School of the University of São Paulo.
  • 1965 - founded a pre-med preparatory course with João Carlos di Genio and other colleagues, where he taught chemistry for
  • 1986 - initiated radio campaigns with the aim of informing the population about AIDS
  • 1989 - volunteered to work as an unpaid physician in one of the largest prisons of Brazil, the Carandiru, in order to tackle the fearsome AIDS epidemics raging among male inmates.
  • 1992 - organized a course on biotechnology in AIDS and cancer
  • 2000 - received the Prêmio Jabuti literary award in 2000 for his book Estação Carandiru.
